Erika Rechtackova

I'm a third-year student at SETU Carlow, studying Interactive Digital Art and Design. I have always been a creative person and dreamed of a creative career. For a long time I thought it wasn't possible for me until I discovered the art of video games. I immediatelly became interested in their creation and the behind the scenes stuff. As I began learning digital art I grew more and more fond of Character creation. Throughout my life I was always making up characters and building their backstories and now I got to build their looks as well.
