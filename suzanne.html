Hippie Movement Informative Website

I created this prototype as part of my UI Testing module in my second year of studying Interactive Digital Art and Design. The goal was to choose a website that we thought could do with some improvements in navigation, looks and aesthetics. I choose a website of the author Suzanne Collins, because her website is very chaotic and unappealing. I made wireframes based on the changes I wanted to make and then made the prototype of how things would look.
